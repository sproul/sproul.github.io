1209 Glen dishwasher

  • Find dishwasher detergent from box under the sink.
  • Open the dishwasher.
  • Pour detergent into the small compartment in the open dishwasher door.
  • Slide the little cover closed over the detergent compartment.
  • Close door.
  • Repeatedly press the 'cycles' button to choose your wash ('normal' takes 2+ hours, 'quick' takes around 1 hour).
  • Press the 'start' button.
