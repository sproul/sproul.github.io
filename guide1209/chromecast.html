1209 Glen chromecast

Control the Chromecast device
  • from your Android phone using the Google Cast app on Google Play
  • OR from your iPhone using the Google Cast app on iTunes
Your phone must be connected to the adyn_pep Wi-Fi network.

Screen mirroring

To mirror your phone on the TV
  • connect to the adyn_pep Wi-Fi network
  • execute Google Cast
  • choose CAST SCREEN/AUDIO
  • select Living Room Chrome Cast
