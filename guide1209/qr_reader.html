QR readers

A QR reader is software that runs on your phone and lets you interpret QR codes like the following:

If you have a QR reader installed, invoke it and point your phone camera at the QR code. If the QR code contains a URL (which is always the case for QR codes at 1209 Glen Ave.), then the QR reader will propose to take you to the web page addressed by that URL, which should have relevant information for you.

Don't have a QR code reader?

There are a number of free QR code readers that work well.
  • Android users can use Google Goggles.
  • iPhone users can use QR reader for iphone.
