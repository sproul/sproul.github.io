Kitchen garbage

The kitchen garbage is in a drawer immediately to the right of the door leading outside. There are two cans, with the rear one for plastic, steel and glass, and the front one for compostables (i.e., everything else). Recyclables we place in a blue container hanging outside the kitchen door.

House garbage

When trash containers in the house get full, we empty them outside to the left of the house in the appropriate containers: blue for recyclables, green for compostable, and black for garbage. We will arrange for these containers to be placed on the street on Wednesday mornings, when the city trucks normally come.
