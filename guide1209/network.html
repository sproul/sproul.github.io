1209 Glen network

If you experience network problems (or any other kind of problem), don't hesitate to contact Nelson at (209) 677-7685.

There are five separate Wi-Fi access points in the house:

  • adyn_pep
  • adyn
  • adyn2
  • adyn3
  • adyn4
They all have the same password -- ahagander -- but these are separate LANs. So if you have multiple devices which need to share a LAN (e.g., a laptop and a portable printer), be sure to connect them to the same named network or else they will not work.

adyn2, adyn3 and adyn4 are plug-in WiFi networks. Normally adyn3 lives in the rear bedroom. adyn2 and adyn4 are usually plugged in upstairs, but can be moved if you prefer. They also accept ethernet if you have wired devices.

All the networks depend on the Comcast cable modem and the adyn_pep router. The adyn network depends also on the adyn linksys router. adyn2, adyn3 and adyn4 also depend on their own internal routers.

The plug-in routers can be restarted simply by pulling them out of the wall and reinserting them.
