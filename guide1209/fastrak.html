1209 Glen fastrak

If you plan on driving across any bridges during your stay, use the FasTrak transponder to avoid lines of cars waiting to pay cash. To use it,
  • let me know your car's license plate state and number, so I can associate your car with the transponder
  • place the device in the middle of your dashboard
  • when you check out, leave it on the piece of furniture by the front door
If you have three or more people in your car (including the driver) and you are crossing the Bay Bridge between 5am and 10am on a weekday, be sure to use the carpool lane to bypass the toll plaza altogether -- on a busy day this route will save you 40 minutes. To get to the bypass,
  • get into the far left carpool lane on 80 as you head south in Berkeley
  • use the leftmost lane marked "San Francisco" to take the flyover toward the bridge
  • take the first right exit marked "BUSES ONLY"
Note that this is not legal if it is not between 5am and 10am on a weekday.
